My Journey in the NoBrackets CTF 2024

An unforgettable experience in one of the most exciting cybersecurity competitions.

Posted by Akasha on November 24, 2024

The NoBrackets CTF 2024 was an exhilarating ride, packed with creative challenges and a competitive atmosphere that truly tested the limits of my skills. This year, I’m thrilled to share that I managed to finish 3rd in the finals—a personal achievement I’m incredibly proud of!

What I enjoyed most were the Pwn challenges. The creativity in challenges like "Locked Websites" and "SuiiiiDo" really stood out. These tasks not only required technical expertise but also pushed me to think outside the box and experiment with new techniques. Every solved challenge felt like uncovering a secret, and that rush is what keeps me coming back to CTFs.

The Challenge Lineup

NoBrackets CTF has a diverse set of challenges spread across categories like Cryptanalysis, Forensics, Reverse Engineering, Web3, and my favorite, Pwn. The qualification round was packed with approachable yet tricky tasks such as "XOR ton prochain" in Cryptanalysis or the cleverly named forensic challenge "Big Browser Is Watching You."

The final round was on another level entirely. Challenges like "VIP Only" and "SuperCat" in the Pwn category demanded not just problem-solving but precision and creativity. The hardware challenges like "Crochetage antivol" added a refreshing twist, combining practical skills with cybersecurity knowledge.

Why NoBrackets Stands Out

Beyond the technical challenges, what makes NoBrackets CTF special is the community and the event's educational focus. The organizers from Galette Cidre CTF did an outstanding job creating an inclusive environment, from providing resources for beginners to crafting challenges that even veterans found engaging.

The Discord server was buzzing with activity throughout the event, serving as a hub for discussion, support, and the occasional banter. It was a place where competitors shared knowledge and celebrated breakthroughs, reinforcing the collaborative spirit that defines ethical hacking.

Looking Ahead

Participating in NoBrackets CTF 2024 has only deepened my love for cybersecurity and CTFs. I’m already looking forward to next year, hoping to improve my skills even further and perhaps aim for that top spot. To anyone considering joining a CTF—don’t hesitate! It’s a challenging but incredibly rewarding experience.

Until then, happy hacking!
